Sessions


Abbotts Ann Nursery School is open for morning and afternoon sessions Monday to Friday. We are open term time only.



You can choose flexible hours between 9:00 - 15:00. 
(PLEASE NOTE that we have had to change our Opening Hours from 1st June 2020 due to COVID-19)

Your child can bring a packed lunch to enjoy at the nursery if they stay past 12pm.  


Hourly Rate: £6.30 for over 3s / £6.85 for 24-36 months

Additional extra sessions can be accommodated where possible at a slightly higher hourly rate. 

Hampshire pre-school funding (15 hours) available for over 3s plus eligible over 2s.
